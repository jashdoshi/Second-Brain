## Campaign Overview

This brief governs the coordinated launch of Meridian Retail Group's refreshed brand identity across all active channels. The campaign — titled internally as *"Crafted for How You Live"* — introduces Meridian's updated visual identity (logo suite, color system, typography; see Brand Guidelines v2.0, approved August 28, 2024) alongside a repositioned brand voice and a new content direction aimed at a premium mid-market lifestyle audience.

The campaign runs from September 16, 2024 through December 31, 2024, with a concentrated launch sprint in the first four weeks.

---

## Target Audience Personas

Three audience personas were developed by Claire Whitmore and approved by Jeff Purdy on July 19, 2024. All campaign creative has been built with these personas as the primary lens.

**Persona 1 — "The Deliberate Nester"**  
Female, 31–44, HHI $90K–$140K. Rents or recently bought her first home. Makes considered, researched purchases. Reads shelter magazines and saves to Pinterest boards obsessively. Values quality over quantity and is willing to wait for the right piece. Shops Meridian for core furniture and kitchen goods. Key message: *Meridian is for the purchase you actually keep.*

**Persona 2 — "The Practical Upgrader"**  
Male/female, 38–52, HHI $110K–$180K. Dual-income household, kids in middle or high school. Tired of replacing things. Has been burned by fast-furniture before and now prioritizes durability and aesthetic longevity. Doesn't follow trends. Key message: *Meridian makes things that earn their place.*

**Persona 3 — "The Gift-Giver"**  
Female, 28–48, wide income range. Shopping for someone else — a housewarming, a wedding, a milestone. Needs the brand to signal quality at a glance. Will not research deeply; the brand itself must close the sale. Key message: *When it has to be right.*

---

## Messaging Pillars

Developed by Claire Whitmore. All campaign copy should ladder to at least one pillar.

1. **Craftsmanship Made Accessible** — Meridian's products reflect genuine craft and consideration, without requiring a luxury price point.
2. **Designed for Longevity** — Aesthetic and physical durability. Pieces that belong in a home for years, not seasons.
3. **A Brand That Earns Trust** — Transparency, consistency, and a shopping experience that doesn't disappoint.

**Campaign Tagline:** *"Crafted for how you live."*  
*(Approved by Diane Castellano, July 18, 2024)*

## Creative Direction Summary

All campaign creative executes under the approved Brand Guidelines v2.0 (final delivered August 28, 2024, by Nadia Osei). Key parameters:

- **Color:** Primary Warm Ivory (PMS 9183 C), Charcoal Grove (PMS 419 C), Dust Rose (PMS 698 C) as accent
- **Typography:** Canela Text for headlines; Suisse Int'l for body and UI
- **Photography direction:** Natural light, lived-in environments, products in use — not studio still life
