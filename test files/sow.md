## 1. Scope of Services

Starry Moore Creative LLC ("Agency") agrees to provide the following services to Meridian Retail Group Inc. ("Client") in connection with the Brand Refresh Campaign as outlined in Proposal SMC-2024-MRG-001, dated May 14, 2024:

- Brand identity refresh, including logo suite refinement and brand guidelines document (v2.0)
- Campaign strategy, including audience persona development and messaging pillars
- Campaign creative production, including OOH executions, social content suite, and email template redesign
- Campaign brief finalization and post-launch analytics framework

Services explicitly excluded from this SOW: media buying, paid social management, web development beyond static asset delivery, and photography or video production.

## 3. Revision Policy

Each deliverable is subject to **two (2) rounds of revisions** at no additional cost. A revision round is defined as a single consolidated set of feedback delivered via a written document or email within five (5) business days of delivery. Feedback received after five (5) business days, or requests for a third revision round, will be scoped and billed at Agency's standard rate of **$195/hour**.

Revision requests must be directed to Marcus Linde (m.linde@starrymoore.com) and Diane Castellano (d.castellano@meridianretail.com) jointly.

---

## 4. Intellectual Property

Upon receipt of final payment (Milestone M4), all original creative work produced under this SOW — including but not limited to logo files, campaign visuals, email templates, and brand guidelines — shall transfer to Meridian Retail Group Inc. as a **full work-for-hire assignment**.

Agency retains the right to display completed work in its portfolio and marketing materials, subject to Client's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.

Agency retains ownership of all pre-existing tools, processes, frameworks, and methodologies used in delivery. Any third-party licensed assets incorporated into deliverables (fonts, stock imagery) remain subject to their respective license terms, which will be documented and delivered to Client alongside final files.

---

## 5. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to treat all shared materials — including the Client's customer segmentation data, brand perception research, and financial information, and the Agency's proprietary strategic frameworks — as confidential. This obligation survives termination of this SOW for a period of **three (3) years**.

---

## 6. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with **30 days written notice**. In the event of termination by Client, Agency is entitled to payment for all work completed to date plus a kill fee equal to **20% of the remaining unbilled contract value**. In the event of termination by Agency for Client non-payment exceeding 30 days past due, the same kill fee applies in reverse.
