## Executive Summary

Starry Moore Creative is pleased to present this proposal for a comprehensive brand refresh campaign for Meridian Retail Group. After an initial discovery conversation on May 9, 2024, we understand that Meridian is looking to reposition itself as a premium mid-market retailer in the home goods and lifestyle space — differentiating from competitors like HomeBase and The Dwelling Co. while retaining brand recognition with its existing customer base.

This proposal outlines a full creative and strategic engagement to be executed across Q3 2024, culminating in a coordinated campaign launch on September 16, 2024.

---

## Project Scope

The engagement covers four core workstreams:

1. **Brand Identity Refresh** — Logo refinement, updated color palette, typography system, and brand guidelines document (v2.0)
2. **Campaign Creative Development** — Hero visuals, OOH executions, social content suite, and email design templates
3. **Messaging & Positioning** — Revised tagline, messaging pillars, and audience persona documentation
4. **Campaign Launch Support** — Final asset delivery, channel deployment guidance, and post-launch analytics framework

Excluded from scope: media buying, paid social management, web development beyond asset delivery.
