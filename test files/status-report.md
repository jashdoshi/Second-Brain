## 4. Delay Flag — D1: Brand Guidelines Document v2.0

**Reason:** During the Phase 1 review meeting on July 22nd, Diane Castellano and Jeff Purdy requested that the brand guidelines expand to include a digital accessibility section (color contrast standards, minimum type sizes for web) and a co-branding chapter covering Meridian's private label sub-brand, *Meridian Home*. These additions were not in the original scope for D1.

Nadia Osei assessed the additional work at approximately 12–14 hours. Per the SOW revision policy, this is within the first revision round and is being absorbed at no additional cost. The delay is isolated to D1 and does not currently impact the campaign launch date of September 16, 2024.

**Mitigation:** Brand guidelines delivery pushed to August 23rd. All downstream deliverables that depend on the guidelines (D5, D6, D7) have sufficient buffer. No change to final milestone dates.
